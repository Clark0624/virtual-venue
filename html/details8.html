The Fault in Our Stars* is a young adult novel by John Green that tells the story of Hazel Grace Lancaster, a sixteen-year-old cancer patient. Hazel is forced to attend a support group where she meets Augustus Waters, a charming cancer survivor.

Hazel and Augustus quickly form a bond and embark on a whirlwind romance. They travel to Amsterdam to meet the author of a book that has deeply affected Hazel, and they face the realities of their illnesses together. The novel explores themes of love, loss, and the search for meaning in the face of mortality. It is a poignant and moving story that has resonated with readers of all ages.
