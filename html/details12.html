"Cars 3," Lightning McQueen, a seasoned champion, faces a new generation of high-tech racers who threaten to leave him in the dust. As he struggles to adapt to the changing landscape of motorsports, McQueen finds himself questioning his legacy and the meaning of his racing career.

With the help of his loyal friend Mater and a brilliant young race technician, McQueen embarks on a journey of self-discovery, pushing his limits and rediscovering his passion for the sport. Along the way, he learns valuable lessons about teamwork, perseverance, and the importance of embracing change.
