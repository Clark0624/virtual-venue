Virtual Venue
Project Of Group 3

Virtual Venue is an innovative company focused on revolutionizing the movie ticket purchasing process by integrating food and beverage deals with ticket sales.

With the goal of providing a seamless experience, Virtual Venue allows users to book tickets and pre-order snacks in one transaction.

The company's mission is to enhance the movie-going experience by saving customers time, offering convenience, and delivering exclusive food promotions.
