"Despicable Me 4," Gru and his Minions are back, facing their biggest challenge yet: a mysterious new villain who threatens to plunge the world into chaos. While Gru grapples with the complexities of fatherhood and trying to maintain a semblance of normalcy, his mischievous Minions stumble upon a secret society of supervillains, eager to recruit them for their nefarious plans.

With a mix of hilarious antics, heartwarming moments, and high-stakes action, the Minions find themselves caught in a whirlwind of espionage, adventure, and self-discovery. As Gru and his Minions navigate this treacherous landscape, they must learn to work together, embrace their unique talents, and ultimately choose the side of good.
