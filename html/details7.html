The Wild Robot is a children's novel by Peter Brown. It tells the story of Roz, a newly manufactured robot who becomes a castaway on an uninhabited island after a shipwreck.

Roz must learn to adapt to the wild surroundings, befriends the local wildlife, and raises a baby goose as her son.
